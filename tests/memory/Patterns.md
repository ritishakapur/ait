# Patterns.md

## Purpose
Patterns that keep emerging across analyses. What this tells us about the business and how to work.

## Format
- **Pattern:** [What keeps happening]
- **Evidence:** [Where we've seen it]
- **Implication:** [What this means]
- **Action:** [What we should do about it]

## Examples
### Pattern: Claims vs Evidence Gap
- **Evidence:** Found in Company.md comparison tables across multiple analyses
- **Implication:** Companies consistently overstate their capabilities or impact
- **Action:** Always build the Claim vs Evidence section — it's the most valuable part of the analysis

### Pattern: Informal Roles Matter More Than Formal Ones
- **Evidence:** Super-VLEs, WhatsApp groups, peer trainers — all emerged organically
- **Implication:** The real system operates outside the org chart
- **Action:** Always include informal/organic roles in stakeholder and org tree analysis

### Pattern: Revenue is Concentrated
- **Evidence:** 80% of revenue typically comes from 20% of streams
- **Implication:** Focus optimization on the high-density revenue streams first
- **Action:** Always do a density analysis on revenue before optimizing
