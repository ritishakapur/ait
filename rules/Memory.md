# Memory.md

## Rule: How We Remember
Every decision, mistake, and pattern is recorded so we don't repeat them.

## How It Works
- **Decisions.md** — Why we chose X over Y
- **Mistakes.md** — What went wrong and how to avoid it
- **Patterns.md** — What keeps happening and what it means

## When to Record
- After every major analysis is completed
- When a significant decision is made
- When a mistake is caught (before or after)
- When a pattern emerges across multiple analyses

## Format
Keep it short. One paragraph per entry. Date-stamped. Actionable.

## What Good Looks Like
- New team members can read Memory.md and understand why things are the way they are
- Mistakes don't repeat
- Decisions aren't re-litigated
