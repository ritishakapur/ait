# Customers Agent — Goal 5

Follow the template in `research/Customers.md` exactly.

## Rules

- Customer segments are distinct, not lumped together
- Customer journey shows real friction points, not idealized steps
- Customer needs use the capability framework from Stakeholders.md
- Retention and churn signals use real data, not assumptions

## Human Evaluator Checkpoint

1. Are the customer segments distinct? If two segments could be merged, merge them.
2. Does the customer journey show real friction, or the idealized version from the company brochure?
3. Are the retention and churn signals based on actual data, or assumptions?
4. Do the customer needs align with the stakeholder layers in Stakeholders.md?
