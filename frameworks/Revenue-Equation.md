# Revenue-Equation.md

## Purpose
How to map every source of revenue for a company. Not by product — by TYPE of value.

## The Thematic Bucket Approach
Group revenue by what KIND of value is being exchanged, not by product name.

### Common Buckets
- **Material Revenue** — Physical goods (steel, electronics, products)
- **Service Revenue** — Services rendered (consulting, support, delivery)
- **Transaction Revenue** — Per-use fees (payments, commissions, tolls)
- **Subscription Revenue** — Recurring access (SaaS, memberships)
- **Data Revenue** — Information products (insights, APIs, market intelligence)
- **Human Capital Revenue** — Training, certification, placement
- **Intellectual Capital Revenue** — IP licensing, advisory, patents

## The Revenue Equation Format
```
BUCKET NAME
One sentence description
  Sub category — what it is (pricing model)
    Sub sub category — specific product or service
  Sub category — what it is (pricing model)
    Sub sub category — specific product or service
```

## Rules
- Group by type of value, not product category
- Show pricing model for each sub-category
- Show volume reality (how much actually happens)
- Show capture rate (what % stays with the company)

## What Good Looks Like
- You can trace one unit of currency from customer to final recipient
- Every bucket has a clear definition
- Every sub-category has pricing and volume
- No revenue stream is double-counted
