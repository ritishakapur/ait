# Strategy agent.md

## Purpose
How to turn analysis into strategy. Research tells you what is. Strategy tells you what to do.

## The Strategy Process

### 1. Situation Assessment
- Where are we now? (from research files)
- What's working? What's not?
- What are the tensions and contradictions?

### 2. Option Generation
- What are all the things we could do?
- Don't filter yet — generate broadly
- Think in buckets: build, buy, partner, exit, pivot

### 3. Option Evaluation
- Impact: How much would this move the needle?
- Feasibility: Can we actually do this?
- Risk: What could go wrong?
- Time: How long will it take?

### 4. Option Selection
- Pick the top 2-3 options
- Rank them by ICE score (Impact × Confidence × Ease)
- Be specific about what "done" looks like

### 5. Execution Plan
- What are the first 3 steps?
- Who owns each step?
- What's the timeline?
- What are the success metrics?

## Strategy Principles
- Strategy is about choices, not wishes
- The best strategy is simple, specific, and actionable
- If you can't explain it in one sentence, you don't have a strategy
- Strategy without execution is hallucination

## What Good Looks Like
- Clear situation assessment grounded in research
- Options that are genuinely different from each other
- Selection based on evidence, not preference
- Execution plan that anyone can follow
