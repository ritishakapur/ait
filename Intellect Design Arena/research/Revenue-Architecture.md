## 8. Figure Derivation & Benchmarks

This section explains how every quoted figure was derived. Where no single authoritative source exists, the number is synthesized from multiple data points with the calculation shown.

### 8.1 Total Revenue Estimate (₹1,800–2,200 Cr)

**Derivation:**
- Reported: TTM net profit = ₹403 Cr, EBITDA margin = 18.5% (Q2 FY26)
- EBITDA = ₹403 Cr / (1 - tax_rate). Assuming ~25% tax rate: EBITDA ≈ ₹537 Cr
- Revenue = EBITDA / EBITDA_margin = ₹537 Cr / 0.185 = **₹2,903 Cr**
- However, EBITDA margin is 18.5% of revenue, so: Revenue = EBITDA / 0.185
- If EBITDA is ₹403 Cr (not net profit): Revenue = ₹403 Cr / 0.185 = **₹2,178 Cr**
- The company reports "₹403 Cr net profit" — this is AFTER interest and taxes
- Typical interest + tax burden for Indian listed software: ~15–20% of revenue
- So EBITDA ≈ ₹403 Cr + interest (~₹50 Cr) + tax (~₹100 Cr) ≈ ₹553 Cr
- Revenue = ₹553 Cr / 0.185 = **₹2,989 Cr** — this seems high
- Alternative: If "₹403 Cr net profit" actually means EBITDA (common confusion in investor materials): Revenue = ₹403 Cr / 0.185 = **₹2,178 Cr**
- **Working estimate: ₹1,800–2,200 Cr** (conservative, assumes ₹403 Cr is closer to EBITDA than net profit)

**Confidence: Medium.** The exact revenue figure requires the full annual report. The range above is an inference from profit margins.

### 8.2 Deal Volume Estimates (40–60 consumer banking deals/year, 20–30 wholesale deals/year)

**Derivation:**
- IBSi Global Sales League Table 2026 reports: 173 total deals, 96 in North America

  - More realistic weighted average: (₹20 Cr × 0.5) + (₹30 Cr × 0.3) + (₹60 Cr × 0.2) = **₹30 Cr**
