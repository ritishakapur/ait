**Deployment:** On-premise, hybrid cloud, multi-cloud (AWS, Azure, GCP)

## 7. Product Strategy & Roadmap

### Current Product Strategy

1. **Composability over monolith** — All products built on eMACH.ai microservices architecture, enabling modular deployment and independent scaling.

2. **AI-first, not AI-add-on** — Purple Fabric is positioned as the intelligence layer across all products, not a separate product. This creates a unified AI strategy.

3. **Subscription model expansion** — Purple Fabric's monthly subscription model signals a structural shift from upfront licensing toward recurring revenue.

4. **Niche dominance** — Dedicated solution suites for Islamic banking, NBFCs, central banking, and credit unions create defensible positions.

5. **Neo-bank expansion** — Dedicated Neo & Digital Bank suite targets the fastest-growing customer segment (digital-only banks).
