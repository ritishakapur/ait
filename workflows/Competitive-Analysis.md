# Competitive-Analysis.md

## Purpose
Deep-dive into competitors — not just who they are, but what they're doing, why it matters, and how to respond.

## Steps

### 1. Competitive Set Definition
- Who are the direct competitors? (from Competition.md — Part A)
- Who are the indirect competitors?
- Who are the adjacent players?
- Who are the substitutes?

### 2. Competitor Deep Dives
For each major competitor:
- What do they actually do? (not what they say)
- What's their business model?
- What's their market share?
- What are their strengths?
- What are their weaknesses?
- What are they doing right now? (last 12 months)

### 3. Positioning Analysis
- How do players compare on key dimensions? (from Competition.md — Positioning Map)
- Where is the whitespace?
- Where is the company positioned?

### 4. Competitive Response Simulation
- If the company launches X, what will competitors do? (from Competition.md — Section 5)
- If a new entrant appears, how will the company respond?
- What's the likely competitive dynamics over the next 12-24 months?

### 5. Strategic Recommendations
- Where should the company compete?
- Where should it not compete?
- What's the competitive advantage to build?
- What's the defensive move?

## Output
A competitive analysis with strategic recommendations.
