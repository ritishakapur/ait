# GTM.md — Go-To-Market Workflow

## Purpose
Analyze how the company reaches its customers and how it could reach new ones.

## Steps

### 1. Current GTM Analysis
- How does the company acquire customers today? (from Customers.md — Acquisition Channel)
- What channels are most effective?
- What's the cost of acquisition per channel?
- What's the conversion rate at each step?

### 2. GTM Gaps
- What customer segments are underserved? (from Customers.md)
- What channels are unused?
- What messaging doesn't resonate?

### 3. GTM Opportunities
- What new channels could the company use?
- What new segments could it reach?
- What partnerships could accelerate growth?

### 4. GTM Recommendations
- Prioritized list of GTM improvements
- Estimated impact of each
- Implementation timeline
- Resource requirements

## Output
A GTM strategy document with actionable recommendations.
