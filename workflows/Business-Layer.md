# Business-Layer.md

## Purpose
This workflow covers the business model analysis — how the company makes money, who it serves, and where it's headed.

## Steps

### 1. Business Model Canvas
Fill out each block of the Business Model Canvas:
- Customer Segments (from Customers.md)
- Value Propositions (from Products.md)
- Channels (from Customers.md — Acquisition Channel)
- Customer Relationships (from Company.md — Core Assets)
- Revenue Streams (from Revenue-Architecture.md)
- Key Resources (from Company.md — Core Assets)
- Key Activities (from Technology.md — Stacks)
- Key Partnerships (from Company.md — Core Assets)
- Cost Structure (from Revenue-Economics.md — Unit Economics)

### 2. Strategic Position
- Where does the company sit in the industry? (from Industry.md)
- What are its moats? (from Competition.md)
- What are its vulnerabilities? (from Competition.md)

### 3. Growth Levers
- What can the company do to grow? (from Revenue-Economics.md — Revenue Levers)
- What's realistic vs aspirational?
- What's the timeline?

### 4. Risk Assessment
- What could go wrong? (from Competition.md — Vulnerabilities)
- What's the company doing about it? (or not doing)
- What's the impact if it goes wrong?

## Output
A one-page business model summary that can be shared with stakeholders.
