# Gap-Analysis.md

## Purpose
Identify what the company is missing — in products, capabilities, market position, or strategy.

## Steps

### 1. Product Gaps
- What do customers need that the company doesn't offer? (from Products.md — Product Gaps)
- What do competitors offer that the company doesn't? (from Competition.md — Gap Analysis)
- Priority: High/Medium/Low

### 2. Capability Gaps
- What skills, technology, or infrastructure does the company lack? (from Technology.md — Technology Gaps)
- What can be built vs what must be bought vs what must be partnered?

### 3. Market Gaps
- What customer segments is the company not serving? (from Customers.md)
- What geographies is the company not covering? (from Company.md)
- What use cases are unaddressed?

### 4. Strategic Gaps
- Where is the company pulling in two directions? (from Company.md — Core Tensions)
- Where is the company's messaging inconsistent? (from Company.md — Contradictions)
- What strategic choices has the company avoided making?

### 5. Gap Prioritization Matrix
| Gap | Impact | Effort to Fix | Priority | Owner | Timeline |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| | High/Med/Low | High/Med/Low | P1/P2/P3 | | |

## Output
A prioritized gap analysis with owners and timelines.
